Network nodes/Brain areas: displays the labeling of the brain parcellation scheme (e.g. AAL atlas) that you defined with “The “BrainRegions” file ”. By default all nodes that are
indicated with a “1” in the first column (“BrainRegions.xlsx” file) will be selected as being part of the network.
However, you can simply redefine your network (e.g. for sub-network analyses) by selecting nodes
of your choice with the mouse cursor (mouse + ctrl).
